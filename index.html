The Princess Charlotte Project

The Princess Charlotte project is an organization started in memory of a special girl named Charlotte O'Shea who lived and loved being a princess in her six years of life. In her memory we collect new dresses, shoes, wands, princess accessories and super hero costumes and distribute them to girls and boys in the waiting room at hospitals.
