The Princess Charlotte Project

The Princess Charlotte project is an organization started in memory of a special girl named Charlotte O'Shea who lived and loved being a princess in her six years of life. In her memory we collect new dresses, shoes, wands, princess accessories and super hero costumes and distribute them to girls and boys in the waiting room at hospitals.

About Us

The Princess Charlotte Project was started by Janet O’Shea in memory of her daughter Charlotte O’Shea who endured four plus years of aggressive cancer treatments for AT/RT brain cancer. During her six years of life, Charlotte spent a lot of time at the Jimmy Fund Clinic and at Children’s Hospital. She loved dressing up as a princess and engaging in imaginative play even while inpatient at the hospital. Charlotte was a social and happy little girl who loved her family, friends, pets, school, dancing lessons and the Red Sox. Despite the difficult treatments she endured she managed to accomplish so much, made friends wherever she went and kept a smile on her face. Charlotte was truly a brave princess!
